openEHR Day April 2018 Plymouth Videos/presentations

Events | May 11, 2018, 7:21 p.m.

Dave Kilroy of appInsight has kindly put together a sneak preview pointing to the materials from the openEHR Day held in Plymouth, UK in April 2018. We will be getting these up into openEHR web pages and You tube channel in due course.

Opening Remarks
  • Presenter: Andy Blofield - Director of IM&T (CIO), University Hospitals Plymouth NHS Trust
  • Recorded Video: Opening Remarks (duration 02:22)
The impact of open standards on global EMR's
  • Presenter: Adrian Byrne - Director of Informatics at University Hospitals Southampton NHS Foundation Trust and chair of the national CIO network
  • Presentation Slides: The impact of open standards on global EMR's
  • Recorded Video: The impact of open standards on global EMRs (duration 40:00)
OPENeP - An Open Standards Approach To An Integrated Digital Care Record
  • Presenter: Andy Blofield - Director of IM&T (CIO), University Hospitals Plymouth NHS Trust
  • Presentation Slides: OPENeP - An Open Standards Approach To An Integrated Digital Care Record
  • Recorded Video: OPENeP - An Open Standards Approach To An Integrated Digital Care Record (duration 30:41)
Innovation in the South West
  • Presenter: Arunangsu Chatterjee – Associate Professor, Director of Technology, Enhanced Learning and Distance Learning, University of Plymouth
  • Presentation Slides: Innovation in the South West
  • Recorded Video: Innovation in the South West (duration 29:46)
The Postmodern EHR
  • Presenter: Tomaz Gornik - Co-chair, openEHR Foundation & CEO, Marand
  • Presentation Slides: The Postmodern EHR
  • Recorded Video: The Postmodern EHR (duration 33:20)
openEHR 101
  • Presenters: Ian McNicoll - Co-chair, openEHR Foundation & CCIO, Inidus and HIldi McNicoll - COO, Inidus
  • Presentation Slides: Ian and Hildi's session was mostly a series of demos, very few slides were involved (hint, watch the video instead)
  • Recorded Video: openEHR 101 (duration 25:19)
The Code4Health Platform and The Apperta Foundation
  • Presenter: Peter Coates - Head of Eco-System Development, NHS Digital
  • Presentation Slides: The Code4Health Platform and The Apperta Foundation
  • Recorded Video: The Code4Health Platform and The Apperta Foundation (duration 20:27)
SeeEHR
  • Presenters: Jason Scott – Agile Development Manager, University Hospitals Plymouth NHS Trust and Gareth Kendall, Senior Software Developer, University Hospitals Plymouth NHS Trust
  • Presentation Slides: Jason and Gareth's session was mostly a series of demos, very few slides were involved (hint. watch the video instead)
  • Recorded Video: SeeEHR (duration 18:17)
DiADeM
  • Presenter: Dave Kilroy - Innovation Associate, NHS Digital
  • Presentation Slides: DiADeM
  • Recorded Video: DiADeM (duration 15:23)
